# Incident Response Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines the placeholder incident response expectations for AI system failures, misuse, policy violations, cost anomalies, or operational degradation.

## Incident Categories

- Model quality degradation
- Drift or monitoring alert
- Unauthorized access or policy exception
- Harmful or non-compliant GenAI output
- Cost threshold breach
- Audit evidence gap

## Future Expansion

- Severity model
- Escalation contacts
- Response timeline
- Post-incident review
- Evidence capture requirements
