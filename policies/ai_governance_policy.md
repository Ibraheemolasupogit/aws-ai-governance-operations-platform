# AI Governance Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines the expected governance controls for ML and GenAI systems managed through the platform.

## Scope

Future versions will describe which AI systems, environments, owners, data domains, and deployment types are covered.

## Governance Principles

- AI systems should have clear ownership.
- Risk tiers should be assigned before production deployment.
- Monitoring, auditability, and incident response expectations should be documented.
- Responsible AI and compliance reviews should be traceable.

## Future Expansion

- Control ownership matrix
- Review frequency
- Required evidence
- Exception handling
- Approval workflow
