# Portfolio Summary

## Project Overview

AWS AI Governance Operations Platform is a local-first portfolio project that models the governance and operations layer around ML and GenAI systems. It does not train a model; it shows how AI systems can be inventoried, checked, monitored, risk-scored, reviewed, audited, and reported.

## Senior-Level Positioning

The project demonstrates thinking beyond notebooks and model accuracy. It focuses on ownership, evidence, controls, cloud operating model, risk prioritisation, and executive communication.

## Target Roles

- Data Scientist with cloud governance experience
- AI Engineer
- MLOps Engineer
- LLMOps Engineer
- AI Platform Engineer
- Cloud/AWS Data Scientist
- Responsible AI or AI Governance practitioner

## Key Capabilities Demonstrated

- AI inventory and model catalogue
- Policy checks and risk scoring
- Access review and audit simulation
- Cost and monitoring summaries
- Incident and risk register
- Model cards and evidence reporting
- AWS architecture documentation
- Tests, linting, and modular Python implementation

## AWS Services Mapped

IAM, AWS Organizations, CloudTrail, CloudWatch, AWS Config, S3, SageMaker Model Registry, Bedrock, Budgets, Cost Explorer, EventBridge, Lambda, DynamoDB, Step Functions, and QuickSight.

## Relevance To AI Governance And AI Platform Operations

The project shows how governance signals can be operationalized: policy failures become findings, findings influence risk scores, risk scores and monitoring signals create incidents, and incidents feed evidence packs and executive reports.

## What Makes It Different From A Normal ML Project

Most ML portfolio projects focus on training or prediction. This project focuses on the operating controls needed after AI systems exist: accountability, traceability, monitoring, auditability, cost governance, and responsible AI oversight.

## Interview Explanation

Explain it as a synthetic control plane for AI governance. It demonstrates how you would organize governance evidence around AWS-hosted ML and GenAI systems without needing real cloud credentials or paid infrastructure for the portfolio version.
