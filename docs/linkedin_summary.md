## Medium Post Version

I have been building a portfolio project focused on the operating layer around AI systems: governance, risk, monitoring, auditability, cost, access, and evidence.

The result is a local AWS-oriented AI Governance & Operations Platform for ML and GenAI systems. It generates a synthetic AI inventory, runs governance policy checks, calculates risk scores, reviews access, simulates audit events, monitors cost and model health, creates incident and risk registers, generates model cards, and produces audit and executive reports.

The project does not connect to AWS or create cloud resources. Instead, it demonstrates how the architecture could map to services such as IAM, CloudTrail, CloudWatch, AWS Config, SageMaker Model Registry, Bedrock, Budgets, Cost Explorer, S3, DynamoDB, EventBridge, Lambda, Step Functions, and QuickSight.

This is the kind of work I enjoy: connecting AI engineering, responsible AI, MLOps/LLMOps, cloud operations, and governance into one practical system.
